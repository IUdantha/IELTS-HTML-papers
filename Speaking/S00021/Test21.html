Test 21

Part 1

Hometown

  1. Where is your hometown?
  2. What was it like growing up there?
  3. Has it changed much since you were a child?
  4. What do you like most about living there?
  5. What kinds of things can visitors to your hometown go and see?
  6. Do you think you will live there when you are older?

Offices

  • Do you work in an office?
  • Is it common for people in your country to work in offices?
  • Where do most of your friends work?
  • Would you like to design an office?

Cities

  • Have you ever lived in a city?
  • What do you enjoy most about living in the city?
  • Which city would you like to live in in the future?
  • Do most people in your country live in cities?
