Section 02

Write about the following topic:

You should spend about 40 minutes on this task.

Working abroad can help employees earn a great deal of money and gain more benefits however, some people think this trend has many negative impacts.

Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of working abroad.

Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience.

Write at least 250 words.
