Part 1

Hometown

  1. Where is your hometown?
  2. What was it like growing up there?
  3. Has it changed much since you were a child?
  4. What do you like most about living there?
  5. What kinds of things can visitors to your hometown go and see?
  6. Do you think you will live there when you are older?

Flowers and Plants

  • Do you have a favourite flower or plant? [Why/Why not?]
  • What kinds of flowers and plants grow near where you live? [Why/Why not?]
  • Is it important to you to have flowers and plants in your home? [Why/Why not?]
  • Have you ever bought flowers for someone else? [Why/Why not?]

Technology

  • What seasons do you have in your country?
  • Which is your favourite season?
  • What do you like to do in this season?
  • Is your country popular with tourists in every season?

Part 2

Describe a piece of equipment in your home that is important to you.

You should say:

  • what it is
  • how long you have had it
  • why you chose this particular model or brand

And explain why it is important to you.

Part 3

  • What are the most popular electronic devices in your country at the moment?
  • Which sort of devices do you think will become popular in the future?
  • Do you think electronic devices make life easier or more difficult?
  • Do you think all information on the internet is true?
  • How can people find reliable information on the internet?
  • Do you think the internet is safe for children to use unsupervised?
