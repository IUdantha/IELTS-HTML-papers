Task 01

You should spend about 20 minutes on this task.

You play a team sport with some friends. Last week a member of the team had an accident and wasn't able to play with you. Write a letter to your friend. In your letter:

  • Tell him which team won
  • Describe the weather conditions that day
  • Say how you felt about the match

Write at least 150 words.

You do not need to write any addresses.
Begin your letter as follows;
