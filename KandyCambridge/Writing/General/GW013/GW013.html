ACTIVITY G07

You should spend about 20 minutes on this task.

You are organising a trip to a major city in your country, for a group of students from your university. Write to the Tour Manager, Mr Abraham, of an organization that organizes trips. In your letter, explain:

  • when you want to visit and how long you will stay
  • how many students are in your group
  • and what accommodation you will require

Write at least 150 words.


You do not need to write any addresses
